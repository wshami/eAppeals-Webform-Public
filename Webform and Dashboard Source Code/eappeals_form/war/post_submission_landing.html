Thank you for submitting this Appeal!

What happens next...

The Appeals Program will:

  • Review your submission and the documents you provided in support of this appeal;
  • Request any required documents from the department that issued the notification;
  • Assign this appeal to an analyst for review, analysis, and response.

We will make every effort to respond to you within 60 days; however, it may take longer based on the complexity of this appeal. You can track the progress of your case at any time from our Home Page.

You will receive a confirmation email from eAppeals_DONOTREPLY@hr.lacounty.gov with the case number within an hour, unless you elected to receive the response by U.S. Mail.

Do not send the same information/documents relating to this appeal by mail as it will create a duplicate appeal. If you need to update this appeal or have any questions, please contact the Appeals Program at (213) 738-3934 between 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., Monday through Friday, except on County holidays.

Thank you,

Department of Human Resources, Appeals Program
